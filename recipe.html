Caramel Custard

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 2 cups full cream milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for custard)
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Melt 1/2 cup sugar in a pan until golden and pour into a baking dish (caramel base).
  2. In another pan, warm the milk.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs with 1/2 cup sugar and vanilla extract.
  4. Slowly add warm milk to the egg mixture, stirring continuously.
  5. Pour this custard mixture over the caramel in the dish.
  6. Place the dish in a water bath and bake at 180°C for 40–45 minutes.
  7. Cool and refrigerate. Invert onto a plate before serving.

Enjoy your homemade Caramel Custard!
